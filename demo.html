User Profile

User Profile is a tool to personalize your web experience. You can control how much of your Demographics & Interests you reveal online (from Coarse to Fine). The more you reveal, the more personalized your experience will be. Firefox estimates this information based on your browsing behavior.

You are always in control of the information you reveal. You can adjust your preferences at any time because Mozilla requires websites not to track this information of you.
